  • The boy named CHRISTIANO RONALDO

Ronaldo is a Portuguese, born on the 5th of february in the year 1985, in Medeira, to Jose Dinis Aveiro. His father was a garderner, while his mother was a cook. He is the youngest child among the four children his parent gave birth to; others being a son and two daughters. He was raised a catholic in a poor family at the funchal civil parish of santo Antonio, he was named by his father after the then U S president [Ronald Reagan].

It was heard that he was expelled from school, for throwing a chair at his teacher because the Teacher told him he cannot make it with football. This attitude of his prompted his parents action to let him face football squarely. "we were eight, nine years", former team-mate Ricardo once said. "And at that time, Ronaldo was already a great player."

"What I remember about him is that he was a humble kid. And when he didn't have the ball, he cried. When the team-mates fought, he cried. But he was already a good player. He was faster than average, he already scored a lot of goals and he had great dribbling skills."
"Cristiano really liked winning", santos added "When that didn't happen, Ronaldo cried. So much that he was nicknamed 'crybaby' ".

        Pilar played with Cristiano in the youth teams at Sporting and also at junior level for Portugal - and he says Ronaldo's desire to improve set him apart from other players.

"I always thought that we had several quality players in the Sporting academy," he said. "But that Ronaldo was different because of all the work he put in - not so much during the training sessions, but after the sessions. He would always stay and practice for an extra half an hour on the areas he had most difficulties.

"When I arrived at the age of 13, he had an authority about him that was much greater than the rest of his team-mates. The way he trained, the way he motivated his team-mates, the way he corrected them, his will to win, his will to impress, was superior to all the others.

"We all wanted to play football and to please our parents. But not him - his dream was not only to please his family, but everyone who watched him."

Upon his arrival at Manchester United, Cristiano was given the Iconic jersey number Seven (7), which had been worn by some icons like Beckham, Contana e.t.c.   • Ronaldo's Social Life

        Ronaldo was one of the most well-known sports stars off the field, and numerous studies of athletes popularity showed that he was the most beloved athlete in the world. His extreme popularity made Ronaldo one of the highest paid endorsers in sports history, and in Novemmber 2016 he became the third person to earn a lifetime contract from the sportswear company Nike, the other's being basketball superstars Micheal Jordan and Lebron James. Moreover, he established his own successful "CR7" brand of products that included shoes, underwears, cloths and fragrances.

       Ronaldo's immense marketability was at the center of a legal issue that arose in june 2017. That month prosecutors filled a lawsuit that accused Ronaldo of defrauding the Spanish government of $16.5 million by hiding his image-rights income in Spain from 2011 to 2014. He was accused of having underestimated the income he earned from the sale and licensing of his image rights and the accompanying tax obligations, but Ronaldo denied all allegations. However, in June 2018 he accepted a suspended two-year prison sentence and agreed to pay $21.8 million to the spanish government to settle the case.
